May 1, 2017—Michelle M. Armitstead, Esq. - Career Advisor

Calling All Admitted 1Ls

Congratulations on being admitted to the J. Reuben Clark Law School! We are excited for you to begin this rigorous and valuable education. Preparing for law school can be an overwhelming process. Living arrangements, tuition, class preparation, and book purchases, etc., are all concerns as you transition into law student life. While incoming law students focus on academic preparation for the coming semester, often career and professional development is overlooked as a critical component to becoming a successful law student and eventual professional. The great news is that your professional and career development is something over which you have complete control!
